Awards

here are many film industry awards, including the National Film Awards, Filmfare Awards, and the Cannes International Film Festival: National Film Awards

This award show is held annually in New Delhi and is introduced by the Indian government. The awards recognize excellence in various categories, such as acting, directing, and writing. The awards are categorized into Feature Films, Non-Feature Films, and Books on Cinema. The Feature Films category includes awards like Best Feature Film, Best Direction, Best Actor, and Best Actress. The awards also include technical categories like Best Cinematography, Best Screenplay, and Best Editing. In addition, the awards include a lifetime achievement award called the Dadasaheb Phalke Award, which is named after the father of Indian cinema.
